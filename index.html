What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. These processes include learning, reasoning, and self-correction.

Applications of AI

  • Virtual assistants (like Siri and Alexa)
  • Recommendation systems (like Netflix and YouTube)
  • Autonomous vehicles
  • Healthcare diagnostics
  • Fraud detection

Benefits of AI

AI can increase productivity, reduce human error, provide personalized experiences, and tackle complex problems faster than traditional methods.

The Future of AI

AI continues to evolve, with advancements in general intelligence, robotics, and ethical AI. It promises to reshape industries and redefine the way we work and live.
